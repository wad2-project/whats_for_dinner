About our app

Have you ever had a bad day where you could not decide what food you want to eat? Or do you simply want to adventure with different/random dishes?

Regardless of your reason, now you have the opportunity to do that while leaving the burden of decision to us.

“What’s For Dinner” is a food selection site for those who can’t decide what they want to eat.
